Get & Make Fresh Food
in a Easy Way

LPU Special's is here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy.Also you can oder from our cloud kitchen situated in the college campus We offer recipes and cooking advice for home cooks, by home cooks. Helping create “kitchen wins” is what we’re all about. LPU Special's is founded in 2022 by the cse students Ranit Pal , Nitika & Pratik Raj as a home cooking blog to record their favorite easy and healthy recipes. Today, LPU Special's has grown into a trusted resource for home cooks with more than 1000 tested recipes, guides, and meal plans, drawing over 2000 readers each month from around the world. We’re supported by a diverse group of recipe developers, photographers,Students in culinary course and other creative professionals.
